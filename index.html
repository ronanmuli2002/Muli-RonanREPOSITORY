About PCNL

Our History

Founded in 1990, the Philippine College of Northwestern Luzon has been a beacon of quality education in the region. Starting with just 50 students, we've grown to serve thousands of aspiring professionals each year.

Our commitment to academic excellence and community service has made us one of the most respected institutions in Northwestern Luzon.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that develops competent professionals and responsible citizens committed to the service of humanity.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Northwestern Luzon recognized for academic excellence, research, and community engagement.

Announcements

Enrollment for Second Semester 2025

Enrollment for the second semester of AY 2024-2025 will begin on November 15, 2024. Please prepare all necessary documents.

Posted: October 1, 2024

New students must bring original copies of their Form 138, Good Moral Certificate, and 2x2 ID pictures. Returning students need to settle their accounts before enrollment.

Foundation Week Celebration

Join us for our annual Foundation Week celebration from December 5-9, 2024. Various academic and cultural activities are prepared.

Posted: September 25, 2024

Activities include: Academic competitions, Cultural shows, Alumni homecoming, Sports fest, and Community outreach programs.

Scholarship Applications

Applications for various scholarships (CHED, DOST, Institutional) are now open until November 30, 2024.

Posted: October 5, 2024

Requirements include: Application form, Latest grades, Certificate of good moral character, Proof of family income, and Recommendation letters.

Research Colloquium 2025

The annual Research Colloquium will be held on January 20-22, 2025. Submission of abstracts is until December 15, 2024.

Posted: September 30, 2024

Themes: Innovation in Education, Sustainable Business Practices, Emerging Technologies, and Social Development Studies.
